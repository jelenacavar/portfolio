CORE STORY

Hello, my name is Jelena Cavar. I'm 20 years old girl from Croatia, living in Aarhus; Denmark.

I am passionate about interactive media. We live in the digital age and bringing the design in life has became essential.

I'm studying Multimedia Design and Communication. Most of my projects are connected with my studies; where I learned work in the team and feel the pressure of the deadlines.

I'm really interested in film; as a part of a film festival in my hometown I had chance to learn from professionals. I love to work on post production, especially with sound. My other big hobby is sport, mostly watching it on TV. :)
